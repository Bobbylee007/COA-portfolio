About Me

my biography.

I’m Chukwufumnaya Augustine Osamor a passionate Software Engineer driven by curiosity and a deep love for technology. My journey began in Lagos and Abuja, where I first discovered computers at a young age. From experimenting with Windows systems to exploring mobile data transfers and device customization, I’ve always been fascinated by how technology works behind the scenes. After earning a diploma in Computer Operations, I continued my studies at Modibbo Adama University of Technology (MAUTECH) while working as a Data Administrator. During this period, I immersed myself in self-learning studying web development, graphic design, and engaging with tech communities to sharpen my skills. In 2021, I gained hands-on experience at Wide Com ICT Solutions, working on networking, CCTV installations, solar systems, and frontend development projects including designing and launching websites for local businesses. In 2022, I was selected for the ICT Youth Empowerment Program (ICT-YEP), where I advanced my web development expertise and strengthened my path toward becoming a professional software engineer. Today, I am focused on building scalable web applications, solving real-world problems, and continuously evolving as a developer.
